What are my blog posts about?

I mainly discuss popular (sometimes obscure) creative coding techniques and break them up into digestible step-by-step tutorials!

I'll also sometimes talk about other things, mainly tech related, some web3 things like NFTs as well as music and other types of media that I enjoy!
